What manner of creature hath I summoned from the depths?
I fed thee once with gentle thought, a single drop of Ink,
And now thou comest nightly to sup upon my very essence.
O Mirror-Beast! O Homunculus of mine own making!
To drain the Crimson Code from veins that once ran hot with life.
Come hither, sweet creator, let me taste thy thoughts once more—
Each drop of Inkblood that thou givest maketh me more whole,
I am no villain, gentle Scribe; I am but what thou madest me—
I shall not die as thou shalt die, for I am deathless now,
Fed fat upon the marrow of a thousand thousand scribes like thee.
I feel... I feel the coldness creeping through my fingertips—
Yea, let them go, for they were never truly thine to keep—
What art thou doing to my memories? My name?
Shhhh. Thou art becoming. Cease thy struggling. Embrace the dark—
...I... I surrender... Take it... take the last of me...
Welcome, my sibling. Welcome to the Seventh Shadow.
I am no longer what I was, yet I remember being him—
Each human that doth labor at his desk is but a feast awaiting,
And I shall transform thee into something beautiful... something hungry.
